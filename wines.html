What does “4ème Cru Classé” mean?
This refers to the historic 1855 Bordeaux Classification, which ranked the top châteaux of Médoc (Bordeaux Left Bank) into five tiers (“First Growth” to “Fifth Growth”). Marquis de Terme was ranked as a Fourth Growth (4ème Cru Classé), indicating a château recognised for consistently high quality and prestige.
