About Cave Secrète

I grew up in Burgundy, not far from Chablis, and I’ve always mostly drunk French wine. I keep a private cellar in Burgundy with more than 400 bottles, and over the last ten years I have purchased more than 300 bottles of French wine in Australia.

Since moving to Australia, I’ve seen how difficult it can be to find good French wine at a fair price. Bottle shop prices are often double or triple what you’d pay in France, and many importers focus on wines that fit the Australian palate — riper, bigger, more fruit-forward — rather than classic French styles.

When I first arrived, I didn’t know anything about Australian wine (I still don’t), so I would go to a bottle shop, pick a random bottle, and often end up disappointed.
