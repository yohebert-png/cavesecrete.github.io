Cave Secrète

Private French Wine Club

Cave Secrète is a private French wine club for a small circle of families in Australia, focused on authentic French wines sourced at fair prices.

I grew up in Burgundy, near Chablis, where wine is part of everyday life. Since moving to Australia, I’ve seen how difficult it can be to find good French wine at a fair price. Bottle shop prices are often 50–100% higher than in France, and the quality can be inconsistent.

Cave Secrète exists to quietly bridge that gap. Every bottle is sourced as if I were buying it for my own cellar, and offered at cost + a 10% sourcing fee.

Selections change depending on what I find at auction and through trusted suppliers. Membership remains intentionally small to keep the club personal, discreet, and genuinely useful.

How It Works

Cave Secrète is designed to be simple, personal, and transparent.

Sourcing

  • Only French wines — Burgundy, Bordeaux, Rhône, Loire, Champagne, and other regions.
  • Wines are sourced from auctions, private collections, and trusted importers in Australia.
  • I only buy bottles I would be happy to drink myself or add to my own cellar in Burgundy.

Pricing

  • You pay exactly what I pay at auction or from suppliers.
  • A flat 10% sourcing fee covers time, research, and handling.
  • Before buying, I check retail prices in both Australia and France.
  • I only buy when the pricing offers genuine value compared to France.

Ordering

  1. Browse the current selection on this site.
  2. Text me with the bottles and quantities you would like.
  3. I confirm availability and reserve bottles for you.
  4. Payment is made by bank transfer.
  5. Collection or delivery is arranged directly.

About Cave Secrète

My name is Yoann. I was born and raised in Burgundy, not far from Chablis. Wine has always been part of everyday life — from family meals to visits with local winemakers.

I keep a private cellar in Burgundy with more than 400 bottles, and over the last ten years I have purchased more than 500 bottles at auction in Australia. I mostly drink French wine, and I know how it feels to stand in front of a shelf here, pick a bottle at random, and end up disappointed.

Many people in Australia face the same problem when buying French wine: high prices, confusing labels, and no guarantee that the wine will suit their taste.

Cave Secrète is my way of making French wine a little easier for a small group of families and friends. The principles are simple:

  • Only French wine.
  • Only bottles I would buy for myself.
  • Only when the price is reasonable compared to France.
  • Only cost + 10% sourcing fee.
  • Only for a small, private group.

Everyone’s taste is different. The goal is not to tell people what they should like, but to give them the chance to explore good French wine without bottle-shop mark-ups.

Membership

Cave Secrète is intentionally small to keep things personal and to make sure there is enough wine for everyone.

Membership is by invitation and focused on people who genuinely enjoy French wine and want to explore it more deeply. At the moment, numbers are limited to a small group of families.
