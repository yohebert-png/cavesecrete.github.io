How It Works

The philosophy is simple, transparent, and personal.

Sourcing

  • Only French wines — Burgundy, Bordeaux, Rhône, Loire, Champagne, and more.
  • Purchased through auctions, private collections, and trusted importers.
  • Every bottle is one I would happily buy for myself.

Pricing

  • You pay exactly what I pay.
  • A flat 10% sourcing fee covers research and time.
  • I only buy when the price is fair compared with France.
  • No profit — if someone buys ten bottles, I treat myself to a nice bottle.

Ordering

  1. Browse the current selection.
  2. Text me with your preferred bottles.
  3. I confirm availability and reserve them for you.
  4. Payment via bank transfer.
  5. Collection or delivery arranged directly.

About Cave Secrète

I grew up in Burgundy, not far from Chablis, and I’ve always mostly drunk French wine. I keep a private cellar in Burgundy with more than 400 bottles, and over the last ten years I’ve purchased more than 300 bottles of French wine in Australia.

Since moving here, I’ve realised how hard it can be to find good French wine at a fair price. Bottle shops often charge double or triple the cost in France, and many importers focus on wines tailored to Australian tastes — bigger, riper, and more fruit-driven — rather than classic French styles.

When I first arrived, I didn’t know anything about Australian wine (I still don’t). I would pick bottles at random and often end up disappointed. I realised that Australians must feel exactly the same when trying to choose a French bottle for the first time — unfamiliar labels, inconsistent styles, and very high prices.

Cave Secrète is my way of making French wine a little easier and more accessible for a small group of families and friends. The principles are simple:

  • Only French wine.
  • Only bottles I would buy for myself.
  • Only when the price is reasonable compared to France.
  • Only cost + 10% sourcing fee.
  • Only small quantities — this is not a commercial operation.

Everyone’s taste is different. The goal isn’t to tell people what they should like, but to give them the opportunity to explore quality French wine without bottle-shop mark-ups.

Membership

Cave Secrète is intentionally small. To keep things personal and ensure there is enough wine for everyone, membership is limited to a small number of families.

At the moment, membership is by invitation only. If you’re interested in joining the waiting list, send a text with your name and wine preferences.
